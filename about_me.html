About Me

Growing up in Bangladesh, I loved solving complex mathematical problems and it has not changed after moving to New York ten years ago. My passion for coding started when I took Intro to Computing and Computer Science classes, where I learned how to program. During my undergraduate years, I loved solving complex problems through programming in Mathematica, a comptuational program. After Graduation, I started learning C++, Python and R online to further improve my understanding of coding.

My approach to coding is just like solving a math problem. Innovate, Creativity, Organize, Design and Execute (ICODE). Currently, I am pursuing my postbaccalaureate in Web Programming, where I mastered HTML, CSS, WordPress and SQL. Looking back, I thank myself for being in love with coding at the intro class because otherwise, I would never have known what I am capable of creating and developing.
